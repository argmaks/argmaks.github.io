I am a PhD student in Computer Science at the [University of Cambridge](https://www.cam.ac.uk/), working in [ProrokLab](https://www.proroklab.org/) under the supervision of Prof. Amanda Prorok. I am a member of [Downing College](https://www.dow.cam.ac.uk/). My research aims to design and deploy decentralized multi-robot systems that can be instructed with language.

## Research Interests

My research interests include robot learning, decentralized multi-robot systems, deep reinforcement learning, ad-hoc coordination, and foundation models.

## Background

I come from a mathematics and statistics background, which has shaped how I approach problems and gives me a strong foundation for understanding AI. I completed a BSc in Mathematics, Optimisation and Statistics at [Imperial College London](https://www.imperial.ac.uk/) and an MSc in Statistics at [ETH Zürich](https://ethz.ch/en.html). During my master's, I visited the [Foerster Lab for AI Research](https://foersterlab.com/) at the University of Oxford, where as part of my thesis I built an LLM coding agent (back when Claude Code was not a thing) capable of implementing and training MARL algorithms.
